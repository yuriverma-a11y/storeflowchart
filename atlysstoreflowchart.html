Atlys · UAE store

In-store user journey

End-to-end flow for the UAE pilot: greeter intake, the seated consultation, and the start-application + payment funnel. Passport and the visa triad (citizenship · residence · destination) are captured in the consultation and prefilled into the funnel.

Start / stepDecisionCaptured / savedDeferred follow-upPaymentHandoff / done
01

Greeter flow

Quick intake at the door. Greeter captures basics, customer picks a welcome drink and signs consent, then is handed to the consultation queue. No passport or destination here.

02

Consultation flow

Consultant takes over. Auth via OTP starts the application. Citizenship, residence and passport are captured per traveller (the dashed loop) since all passports are required; destination, briefing and FAQs are shared for the group. The passport "upload later" path fires the app link and 24h / 48h / 7-day reminders in the background while the customer continues.

03

Start-application & payment flow

Funnel opens with triad, auth and passport already prefilled, so it begins at pending documents. Whether docs are scanned on-site or sent for later upload, both paths take payment and produce one combined invoice for the group.

* POS payment is still being scoped — shown alongside payment link and cash as a tentative third method.
Re-engagement reminders (24h · 48h · 7 day) are shared between the passport "upload later" path and the missing-docs path.
